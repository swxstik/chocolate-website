Our Story

ChocoWorld is a women-led entrepreneurial venture that has been delighting chocolate lovers for over 15 years. With a commitment to quality and creativity, we cater to a wide range of clients, including individuals, corporate organizations, sweet shops, bakeries, and other businesses. Currently, we are embarking on an exciting journey to design bite-sized chocolates, offering a perfect balance of flavor and convenience to our customers.

Producing an impressive one ton of chocolate every month, ChocoWorld has become a trusted name in the chocolate industry. Our growth is a testament to the passion of our team and the loyalty of our customers. As we continue to innovate and expand, we remain dedicated to crafting memorable chocolate experiences and fostering strong partnerships within the industry.
